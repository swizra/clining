About Us

Soyth is a leading consulting club in Basel that brings together industry experts, passionate professionals, and ambitious students to explore business strategy, innovation, and leadership.

lightbulb_outline Our Mission

To empower the next generation of consultants by providing a platform for learning, networking, and hands-on experience in the consulting world.

visibility Our Vision

We envision a dynamic community where innovative thinking meets practical expertise, fostering personal and professional growth for all our members.

check_circle_outline Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, and Collaboration are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to create an inclusive and empowering environment for every member.
