FAQ

Have questions? Here’s what you need to know before reaching out.

Who can join?

We welcome individuals who are passionate about consulting, strategy, and professional growth. Our community is built on trust and shared ambition, so we take the time to ensure every new member is the right fit.

Is there a membership fee?

No, we do not charge for membership. Becoming part of this community is about shared goals and meaningful contributions, not financial transactions.

How do I apply?

There is no formal application process. If you're interested, the first step is to reach out and introduce yourself. From there, we have a conversation, and if it feels like a good match, we invite you to experience the community firsthand.

What kind of people are in this community?

Our members come from diverse backgrounds, including consulting, entrepreneurship, and strategy. They are driven professionals who value collaboration, growth, and impactful work.

Can I join if I’m new to consulting?

Yes. Experience is valuable, but so is potential. If you're eager to learn, contribute, and develop your skills, we encourage you to reach out.

What do members do?

Members engage in consulting projects, participate in workshops, connect through networking events, and collaborate on professional development initiatives.

What if the community isn't the right fit for me?

Our goal is to maintain a strong and engaged network. If at any point it feels like the community isn’t the right fit, we understand and respect that.

How can I contact you?

If you'd like to learn more, the best way to start is by reaching out directly. We’re always open to meaningful conversations. Our email: contact@soyth.ch.
